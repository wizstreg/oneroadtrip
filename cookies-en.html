1. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. It allows the site to remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language or navigation choices, to facilitate your next visit.

Cookies may be set by the website you visit ("first party" cookies) or by third-party services ("third party" cookies).

2. Your consent

When you first visit the Website, a banner informs you about the use of cookies and allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies (necessary + optional)
  • Refuse optional cookies (only necessary cookies are set)
  • Customize your choices by category

In accordance with regulations (GDPR and ePrivacy Directive), strictly necessary cookies for website operation are set without prior consent.

No non-strictly necessary cookies (analytics, third-party content, etc.) are placed until you have made your choice via the banner. You can withdraw your consent at any time, as easily as you gave it, via "Manage cookies".
