‚ùì Frequently asked questions

What weather should you expect?

Dry Season (November-April): Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal for all activities, peak tourist season, highest prices.

Shoulder Season (May-June, November): Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate prices, excellent value, occasional brief showers.

Wet Season (July-October): Heavy rainfall, hurricane risk, lowest prices, lush landscapes, indoor activities preferred, some areas inaccessible.

Diving Season: Year-round excellent, dry season best visibility, wet season warmer water temperatures, whale shark season (March-June).

How many days should I plan?

We believe you will enjoy this itinerary best by dedicating between 12 and 18 days. The next step will allow you to adjust the duration of your stay.

What to discover in Belize?

English-speaking Caribbean nation with Maya ruins, barrier reef diving, and jungle adventures. Experience Blue Hole diving, ancient temples, and incredible biodiversity in compact Central American paradise. The country offers authentic Caribbean culture with Maya heritage and pristine natural environments. Belize combines adventure, culture, and relaxation perfectly.
