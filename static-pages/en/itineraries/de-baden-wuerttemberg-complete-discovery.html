Stand in awe before the Speyer Cathedral (Speyer Dom), a monumental Romanesque masterpiece that served as the principal church of the Holy Roman Empire. The cathedral's twin towers and red sandstone facade have dominated the Rhine plain for nearly a thousand years.

Descend into the imperial crypt to pay respects at the final resting place of medieval rulers who once shaped the destiny of Europe. An excellent audio guide available in multiple languages brings the cathedral's fascinating history vividly to life.
3
Visit the museum across the cathedral square to deepen your understanding of Speyer's pivotal role in European history. The collection traces the city's development from Roman times through its prominence in the Holy Roman Empire. In summer, the lively Brezelfest fills the streets with carnival rides and traditional festival tents, while December brings a charming small-scale Christmas market.
