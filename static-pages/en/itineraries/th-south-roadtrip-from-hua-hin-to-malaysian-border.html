Follow the coast from Prachuap and its calm bays, then enter Khao Sok amid emerald lakes and cliffs. Continue to Krabi, limestone terrain and boat-accessible coves, before reaching Koh Libong where the island keeps a discreet rhythm. In the deep south, Thai Muslim culture adds another color to the route.

❓ Frequently asked questions

What weather should you expect?

Cool Season (November-February): Perfect weather (20-30°C) with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures, and peak tourist season. Highest prices but optimal conditions for all activities and sightseeing.

Hot Season (March-May): Very hot temperatures (30-40°C) with moderate crowds and prices. Challenging for extensive outdoor activities but good for beaches and air-conditioned attractions.

Rainy Season (June-October): Heavy afternoon rains, high humidity, but lowest prices and fewer crowds. Lush landscapes, some flooding, but authentic experiences and great value.

How many days should I plan?

We believe you will enjoy this itinerary best by dedicating between 12 and 18 days. The next step will allow you to adjust the duration of your stay.

What to discover in Thailand?

Southeast Asian paradise with ancient temples, incredible cuisine, and vibrant culture. Experience Buddhist traditions, beautiful beaches, and authentic Thai hospitality.
