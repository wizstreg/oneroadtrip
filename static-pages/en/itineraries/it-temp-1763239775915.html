THE MOST BEAUTIFUL VILLAGES OF LOMBARDY: LAKES AND MOUNTAINS

Discover Crespi d'Adda and its industrial heritage frozen in time, then reach Soncino and its mighty ramparts. Stop at the sanctuary of Grazie, stroll through Castellaro Lagusello by the water, breathe in the lemon trees of Limone, climb to Bienno and reach Bormio before following the calm shores of Varenna. Lombardy connects lakes and mountains with elegance.
