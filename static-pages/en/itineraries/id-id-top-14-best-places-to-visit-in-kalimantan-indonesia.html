Borneo's Wild Coast: Kalimantan Jungles, River Safaris & Island Adventures

Islands floating on perfect turquoise, coral reefs teeming with life, rivers carving through jungle. Eastern Kalimantan, hidden treasure.

≈ 22
Days
4311

Depart from Tarakan, gateway to Derawan's clear waters, then follow the Berau coast where reefs and beaches remain preserved. In Tanjung Redeb, fishing traditions and local life intersect, before reaching Maratua and its lagoons open to a peaceful sea horizon.

  • Tarakan: north island gateway
  • Berau: coastal diving heart
  • Maratua: perfect coral islands
  • Kakaban: saltwater lake wonder

❓ Frequently asked questions

What weather should you expect?

Dry Season (May-September): Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, clear skies, peak tourist season, best diving conditions, advance booking recommended.

Shoulder Season (April & October): Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate rainfall, reasonable prices, excellent conditions, transitional seasons, good value.

Wet Season (November-March): Heavy rainfall, lush landscapes, tropical storms, humidity high, lowest prices, fewer tourists, challenging conditions.

Regional Variations: Diverse climates, equatorial zones, monsoon patterns, altitude differences, island variations, weather monitoring essential, seasonal planning.

How many days should I plan?

We believe you will enjoy this itinerary best by dedicating between 18 and 26 days. The next step will allow you to adjust the duration of your stay.

What to discover in Indonesia?

World's largest archipelago with incredible diversity, pristine beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Experience Bali's temples, Java's volcanoes, and authentic Indonesian culture.
