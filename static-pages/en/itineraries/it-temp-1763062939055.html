Discover Pietrabbondante's Samnite theater clinging to the slopes, then listen to Agnone's bronze bells resonate through the valleys. Cross Venafro and its Roman traces, then reach Campobasso and its heights. Molise reveals itself to those who take time, between ancient stone, forests and tables where truffle perfumes the plates.
