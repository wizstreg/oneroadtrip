Royans-Vercors, France: Alpine Villages and Dramatic Gorge Balconies

Stone villages clinging to cliffs, dizzying gorges, flowering meadows carpeting high pastures, endless panoramas from the ridges. Vercors, natural fortress.

≈ 15
Days
156

📖 9 min read 🔄 Last updated: 2026-02-03

Tour Royans-Vercors between gorges and plateaus. Discover Saint-Antoine-l'Abbaye and its monastic heritage, cross Pont-en-Royans suspended above the river, then explore Vercors' vast spaces between meadows and cliffs.

  • Saint-Antoine: monastery limestone heritage
  • Pont-en-Royans: houses cliff overhang
  • Villard-de-Lans: plateau mountain village
  • Vercors Gorges: dramatic rock formations
