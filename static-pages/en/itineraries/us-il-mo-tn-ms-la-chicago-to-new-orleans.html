Route 66 Southern Leg: Chicago Blues to New Orleans Jazz History

Electric blues crackling through night air, clubs pulsing with Delta rhythms, second-line umbrellas twirling in parade. Route 66 heading south.

Days
2068

Connect Chicago to New Orleans following music roads, with a detour through St. Louis and its riverbanks. Make Nashville pulse in concert halls, then dive into Memphis between studios and blues. Then cross the Mississippi Delta, dark bars and electric guitars, before reaching New Orleans and its second-line parades. An itinerary where each stage has its soundtrack.

  • Chicago: electric blues lakefront icon
  • Nashville: songwriter rounds live music
  • Memphis: studio legends river bluffs
  • New Orleans: second-line Crescent magic
