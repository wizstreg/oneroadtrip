South India Explorer: Hampi's Ruins, Goa's Beaches & Nilgiri Tea Hills

Temple ruins rising from boulder chaos, waves pounding golden shores, tea hills rolling green. South India, intoxicating in its diversity.

Discover Bengaluru's buzz, explore Anegundi's history-laden boulders and rest on Patnem beach in Goa. Follow Fort Kochi's canals, climb toward Coimbatore and end among the Nilgiris tea plantations, where southern India connects temples, sea and mountains.

  • Hampi: boulder temple ruins heritage
  • Goa: beach sanctuary tropical escape
  • Nilgiris: tea plantations emerald slopes
  • Kochi: historic harbor colonial architecture

❓ Frequently asked questions

What weather should you expect?

Winter (October-March): Perfect weather, cool temperatures, dry conditions, peak tourist season, ideal touring, clear skies, advance booking essential.

Shoulder Season (April & September): Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate temperatures, reasonable prices, transitional seasons, excellent value.

Summer (April-June): Hot weather, challenging conditions, hill stations popular, lowest prices, fewer tourists, air conditioning essential.

Monsoon (June-September): Heavy rainfall, lush landscapes, romantic atmosphere, lowest prices, challenging travel, cultural experiences, spiritual season.

Regional Variations: Diverse climates, altitude differences, seasonal patterns, weather monitoring, regional planning, flexible scheduling essential.

How many days should I plan?

We believe you will enjoy this itinerary best by dedicating between 14 and 22 days. The next step will allow you to adjust the duration of your stay.
