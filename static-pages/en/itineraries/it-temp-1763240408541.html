Piedmont's Hidden Villages: Langhe, Monferrato & The Alps

Wine-dark hills descending in terraces, medieval towers crowning peaceful villages. Piedmont, rural beauty and tradition.

≈ 20
Days
687

📖 10 min read 🔄 Last updated: 2026-02-03

Cross Garessio's wooded terrain, then reach Chianale's isolated plateau with its end-of-the-world feel. Approach Ostana and its alpine stones, follow vine-covered hills around Monforte d'Alba, explore Guarene and Neive, before feeling Castagnole's wine-country gentleness. Piedmont preserves a deep intact identity.

  • Barolo: wine terraces cascade hills
  • Monforte d'Alba: vineyard village crowns
  • Neive: medieval streets echo quiet
  • Orta San Giulio: lake island charm
