Explore the Twin Cities and their waterfront neighborhoods, then pass through Eden Prairie and its parks and trails. Reach Minnetonka to sail the lake, then end at Chanhassen and its dinner theater, between popular culture and convivial evening. Minnesota is lived in balance: accessible nature, pleasant local life, and plenty of water to breathe.

Key highlights (4)
  • Lake Minnetonka: sailing, summer estates
  • Eden Prairie: trails, corporate campus green
  • Chanhassen: dinner theater, paisley park
  • White Bear Lake: resort town charm
