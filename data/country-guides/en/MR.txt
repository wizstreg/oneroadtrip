# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Cool Season (November-February)::** Best weather with comfortable temperatures (15-28°C), minimal rainfall, and pleasant conditions for desert exploration. Peak season when tourism is most feasible.

**Hot Season (March-May)::** Increasingly hot temperatures (25-40°C) making travel challenging, especially in interior regions. Limited tourist activities due to extreme heat, early morning travel recommended.

**Rainy Season (June-October)::** Brief rains in southern regions, very hot and humid conditions. Some areas may become inaccessible, though temperatures moderate slightly (28-38°C).

**Harmattan Season (December-March)::** Dry dusty winds from Sahara create hazy conditions but cooler desert temperatures. Best time for Saharan experiences when security permits.

## Unique Experiences
Saharan authenticity with traditional nomadic culture, ancient trading posts, and pristine desert landscapes. Experience traditional crafts, camel culture, and authentic African desert experiences. The country offers cultural immersion with natural beauty and historical significance. Mauritania provides authentic Saharan experiences with cultural depth and natural preservation.

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY WARNING:** Mauritania has significant security risks including terrorism threats, kidnapping dangers (especially for foreigners), and regional instability. Many governments advise against travel to border areas with Mali and Western Sahara. Organized tours with security escorts and comprehensive insurance essential. Check current government travel advisories before planning any visit.
