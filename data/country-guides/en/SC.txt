# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather conditions (24-28°C) with minimal rainfall, excellent visibility for diving, and ideal conditions for beach activities. Peak tourist season with highest prices but most comfortable temperatures and trade winds.

**Shoulder Seasons (April, November)::** Excellent weather with light breezes, fewer crowds, and moderate prices. Best value season with pleasant conditions for all activities and good accommodation availability.

**Wet Season (November-March)::** Warmer temperatures (26-30°C) with higher humidity, occasional heavy rainfall, and potential for tropical storms. Lower prices but weather limitations, lush green landscapes, and excellent for nature photography.

**Year-Round Appeal::** Tropical climate with consistently warm temperatures making beach activities possible throughout year. Seasonal variations minimal compared to other destinations.

## Unique Experiences
Indian Ocean paradise with pristine beaches, unique granite formations, and luxury resorts. Experience incredible nature, romantic atmosphere, and beautiful islands. The country offers luxury experiences with natural beauty and cultural authenticity. Seychelles provides ultimate tropical paradise with natural wonders and sophisticated amenities.
