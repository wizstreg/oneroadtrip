# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Peak Season (April-October)::** Warmest weather, perfect for beaches, highest prices, most crowded, ideal swimming conditions.

**Shoulder Season (March-April, November)::** Pleasant temperatures, fewer crowds, good value, excellent for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

**Off Season (December-February)::** Cooler weather (60-70Â°F), lowest prices, perfect for cultural activities, limited water sports.

**Whale Watching Season (March-April)::** Humpback whale migration, excellent viewing opportunities, nature tours available.

## Unique Experiences
Pink sand beaches and crystal-clear waters create the perfect Atlantic island getaway. British colonial charm meets modern luxury with world-class golf courses and shipwreck diving. Experience unique Bermudian culture while enjoying subtropical paradise just hours from major cities. Bermuda offers sophisticated island luxury with convenient accessibility.
