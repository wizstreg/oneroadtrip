# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY NOTE:** While major tourist destinations (Riviera Maya, Puerto Vallarta, etc.) are generally safe with good security, some regions have elevated crime risks due to organized crime. Avoid border areas with high violence rates and check government travel advisories for current regional conditions. Stick to tourist zones and use reputable transportation.

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-April)::** Peak tourist season with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (20-28Â°C), and perfect beach weather. Highest prices but ideal conditions for all activities.

**Shoulder Season (May, October)::** Good weather with brief rains, moderate prices, and fewer crowds. Excellent value while maintaining comfortable conditions for most regions.

**Hurricane Season (June-September)::** Hot, humid weather with potential storms on Caribbean and Pacific coasts. Lowest prices and lush landscapes, but weather monitoring essential.

**Regional Variations::** Northern deserts hot year-round, central highlands mild, coastal areas tropical. Timing depends on specific destinations and preferred activities.
