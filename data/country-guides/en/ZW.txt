# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (April-October)::** Perfect weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (18-28째C), and excellent wildlife viewing conditions. Peak safari season with animals concentrated around water sources, clear skies, ideal for Victoria Falls visits.

**Cool Dry Season (May-August)::** Excellent temperatures (12-24째C) with clear skies, perfect wildlife viewing, and comfortable outdoor conditions. Peak safari season, Victoria Falls full flow, excellent photography, moderate accommodation rates.

**Hot Dry Season (September-October)::** Hot temperatures (25-35째C) with excellent wildlife concentrations but challenging heat conditions. Best game viewing, Victoria Falls reduced flow, fewer tourists, reasonable rates.

**Wet Season (November-March)::** Hot, humid weather (25-35째C) with heavy rainfall creating lush landscapes but limiting access. Bird watching season, difficult road conditions, lowest accommodation rates, dramatic scenery.

Southern African heritage with Victoria Falls, ancient ruins, and incredible wildlife. Experience Great Zimbabwe monuments, traditional culture, and authentic African experiences.
