# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Best weather conditions, minimal rainfall, ideal for outdoor activities, cooler temperatures, peak tourist season.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Higher humidity, more rainfall, cyclone season possible, lush vegetation, fewer visitors, lower accommodation rates.

**Red Crab Migration (October-December)::** Spectacular natural phenomenon, millions of crabs, road closures, unique wildlife experience, plan around migration.
