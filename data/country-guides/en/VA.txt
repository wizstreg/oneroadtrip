# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Spring (April-May)::** Perfect weather (18-25°C) with comfortable temperatures, fewer crowds than summer, and excellent conditions for outdoor areas and walking. Ideal for exploring without extreme heat.

**Autumn (September-October)::** Excellent temperatures with stable weather, fewer tourists than peak season, and comfortable conditions for extensive touring. Good value with moderate crowds.

**Winter (November-March)::** Cool temperatures (8-15°C) with shortest lines, lowest accommodation prices in Rome, and authentic local atmosphere. Indoor attractions perfect for cooler weather.

**Summer (June-August)::** Hot temperatures (25-35°C) with longest days but massive crowds and highest prices. Early morning visits essential to avoid heat and tour groups.

Spiritual center with incredible art, religious heritage, and unique governance. Experience Sistine Chapel, papal audiences, and profound spiritual experiences.
