# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, peak tourist season, highest prices, advance booking essential.

**Shoulder Season (May & November)::** Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate temperatures, reasonable prices, occasional showers, excellent value.

**Hurricane Season (June-November)::** Higher rainfall, storm risk, lush landscapes, lowest prices, fewer tourists, weather monitoring essential.

**Carnival Season (January-March)::** Vibrant celebrations, cultural festivities, crowded accommodations, highest prices, advance booking required, festive atmosphere.

## Unique Experiences
French Caribbean paradise with volcanic landscapes, pristine beaches, and Creole culture. Experience tropical perfection with European standards and authentic Caribbean atmosphere. The islands offer cultural diversity with natural beauty and excellent cuisine. Guadeloupe provides French sophistication with Caribbean paradise and cultural authenticity.
