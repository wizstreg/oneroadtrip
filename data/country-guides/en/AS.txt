# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Less humid, fewer rain showers, best weather for outdoor activities and hiking. Peak tourist season but still relatively uncrowded.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Higher humidity, more frequent rain showers, lush green landscapes. Cyclone season (December-April) brings occasional storms.

**Year-Round::** Tropical climate with temperatures 75-85°F (24-29°C) consistently. Trade winds provide cooling. Ocean temperatures perfect for swimming year-round.

**Cultural Events::** Flag Day (April 17) is major celebration, White Sunday (October) for children, various cultural festivals throughout the year.

## Unique Experiences
Polynesian culture thrives in this Pacific paradise with pristine coral reefs and volcanic landscapes. Traditional villages offer authentic cultural experiences while national parks protect unique ecosystems. Experience fa'a Samoa lifestyle and incredible diving opportunities in uncrowded waters. Perfect for those seeking genuine Polynesian culture and natural beauty.
