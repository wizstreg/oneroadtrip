# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Peak Season (December-March)::** Prime ski season with excellent snow conditions, but most crowded and expensive. Perfect for winter sports enthusiasts.

**Summer Season (June-September)::** Ideal for hiking, mountain biking, and outdoor activities. Pleasant temperatures and long days, but can be busy with hikers.

**Shoulder Seasons (April-May, October-November)::** Best balance of moderate weather, fewer crowds, and reasonable prices. Spring brings wildflowers, autumn offers stunning fall colors.

**Off-Season (Late March-May)::** Ski season ends, hiking season hasn't fully begun. Some mountain huts and high-altitude activities may be closed.

## Unique Experiences
Andorra offers duty-free shopping in the Pyrenees with world-class skiing and hiking trails. Experience unique Catalan culture while enjoying thermal spas and mountain adventures. This tiny principality combines French and Spanish influences with stunning alpine scenery. Perfect for outdoor enthusiasts and shoppers seeking luxury goods without taxes.
