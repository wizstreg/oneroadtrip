# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather with low humidity, minimal rainfall (50-100mm monthly), and comfortable temperatures (20-26Â°C). Peak tourist season with higher accommodation rates but ideal conditions for outdoor activities and island exploration.

**Shoulder Season (April, November)::** Excellent weather with moderate rainfall, fewer crowds, and reasonable prices offering outstanding value. Pleasant temperatures with occasional tropical showers, perfect for diving and cultural experiences.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Higher humidity, increased rainfall (200-300mm monthly), and cyclone risk requiring flexible planning and insurance. Lower accommodation rates, lush landscapes, fewer tourists, but weather-dependent activities.

**Cyclone Season (November-April)::** Pacific cyclone season with peak activity January-March requiring comprehensive travel insurance and flexible bookings. Modern weather tracking provides advance warning, though direct hits can be severe.
