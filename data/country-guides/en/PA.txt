# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY NOTE:** Panama is generally safe in tourist areas, though standard precautions apply regarding petty crime in cities. Avoid displaying valuables, use official transportation, and stay in well-established tourist zones. Some remote border areas with Colombia should be avoided due to security concerns.

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-April)::** Peak tourist season with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (24-32Â°C), and excellent conditions for outdoor activities and beach visits. Highest prices and crowds.

**Shoulder Season (May, November)::** Good weather with brief afternoon showers, moderate prices, and fewer tourists. Excellent value while maintaining pleasant conditions for most activities.

**Green Season (May-November)::** Higher rainfall with lush landscapes, significantly lower prices, and fewer crowds. Morning activities work well before afternoon rains arrive.

**Regional Variations::** Caribbean coast wetter year-round, Pacific coast has distinct dry/wet seasons. Mountain areas cooler with different precipitation patterns than lowlands.
