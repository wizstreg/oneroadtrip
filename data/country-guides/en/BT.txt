# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Spring (March-May)::** Clear mountain views, rhododendrons blooming, excellent weather, ideal for trekking, moderate crowds.

**Fall (September-November)::** Crystal clear skies, perfect weather, best mountain visibility, peak tourist season, highest prices.

**Winter (December-February)::** Clear skies, cold temperatures, fewer crowds, lower valleys comfortable, high passes may be closed.

**Summer/Monsoon (June-August)::** Heavy rainfall, cloudy conditions, lush landscapes, lowest prices, limited mountain views.

## Unique Experiences
Last Shangri-La measures Gross National Happiness while preserving ancient Buddhist culture and pristine Himalayan landscapes. Experience tiger's nest monastery, traditional festivals, and carbon-negative environmental policies. The kingdom offers spiritual journeys and incredible mountain trekking with authentic cultural immersion. Bhutan provides transformative experiences in the world's happiest country.
