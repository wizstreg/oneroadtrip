# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-March)::** Best weather conditions, minimal rainfall, ideal for sightseeing and cultural activities, peak tourist season.

**Harmattan Season (December-February)::** Dry desert winds, dusty conditions, cooler temperatures, clear skies, excellent for photography.

**Shoulder Season (April-May, October)::** Moderate weather, fewer crowds, good value, transitional periods, pleasant conditions.

**Wet Season (June-September)::** Heavy rainfall, high humidity, lush landscapes, challenging road conditions, fewer tourists, lower prices.
