# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Peak Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather with low humidity, minimal rainfall, trade winds provide cooling. Most expensive period but ideal conditions.

**Shoulder Season (May-June, November)::** Good weather with occasional showers, fewer crowds, better prices, still pleasant for beach activities.

**Hurricane Season (July-October)::** Higher humidity, more rainfall, potential storms, but lowest prices and fewer tourists. September-October highest hurricane risk.

**Sailing Season (December-May)::** Prime time for yachting and sailing, Antigua Sailing Week (April/May), steady trade winds.

## Unique Experiences
365 beaches await discovery on these Caribbean islands with crystal-clear waters and coral reefs. Nelson's Dockyard offers fascinating naval history while local festivals showcase vibrant culture. Perfect sailing conditions and friendly locals create an authentic Caribbean experience. Ideal for beach lovers and history enthusiasts alike.
