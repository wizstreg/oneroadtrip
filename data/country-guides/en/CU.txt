# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-April)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures, peak tourist season, higher prices.

**Wet Season (May-October)::** Higher humidity, afternoon showers, hurricane season, fewer crowds, lower prices, lush landscapes.

**Winter (December-March)::** Coolest temperatures, ideal weather, highest prices, most crowded, advance booking essential.

**Hurricane Season (June-November)::** Monitor weather, September-October peak risk, travel insurance important, potential disruptions.

## Unique Experiences
Time capsule island with classic cars, revolutionary history, and infectious music culture. Experience Havana's colonial architecture, salsa dancing, and authentic Caribbean atmosphere. The country offers unique socialist culture with beautiful beaches and rich heritage. Cuba provides authentic cultural experiences in politically unique Caribbean nation.
