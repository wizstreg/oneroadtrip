# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY WARNING:** Generally safe but exercise caution due to petty crime in cities, armed robbery in remote areas, and border tensions with some neighboring countries. Avoid displaying valuables, use reputable tour operators for wildlife activities, and be cautious in northern regions near South Sudan border.
