# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Peak Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, trade winds, highest prices, most crowded, advance booking essential.

**Shoulder Season (May-July, November)::** Excellent weather with occasional showers, good value, fewer crowds, ideal for diving and outdoor activities.

**Hurricane Season (August-October)::** Lower risk than other Caribbean islands, occasional storms, lowest prices, fewer tourists.
