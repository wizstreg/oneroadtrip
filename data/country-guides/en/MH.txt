# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-April)::** Best weather with lower humidity, minimal rainfall, and trade winds. Peak season for diving and water activities with clearest visibility and calmest seas.

**Trade Wind Season (January-March)::** Consistent northeast trade winds provide comfortable temperatures (26-29Â°C) and excellent sailing conditions. Ideal for water sports and outdoor activities.

**Shoulder Season (May-June, November)::** Good weather with brief showers, fewer visitors, and moderate conditions. Still excellent for diving and cultural experiences with comfortable temperatures.

**Wet Season (July-October)::** Higher rainfall and humidity, typhoon season with potential storms. Lowest tourist numbers but lush landscapes and dramatic skies for photography.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific paradise with pristine atolls, World War II history, and traditional culture. Experience authentic island life, incredible diving, and Marshallese hospitality. The country offers untouched natural beauty with cultural authenticity and historical significance. Marshall Islands provide genuine Pacific experiences with cultural preservation and natural wonders.
