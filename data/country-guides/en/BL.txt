# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Peak Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather, low humidity, minimal rainfall, highest prices, most crowded, advance booking essential.

**Shoulder Season (May-June, November)::** Excellent weather with occasional showers, fewer crowds, better rates, ideal balance.

**Off Season (July-October)::** Hurricane season risk, higher humidity, lowest prices, many restaurants close, September-October highest storm risk.

**Festival Season (December-January)::** New Year celebrations, yacht gatherings, vibrant atmosphere, premium pricing, international crowd.

## Unique Experiences
Luxury Caribbean destination with French sophistication, pristine beaches, and exclusive resorts. Experience high-end shopping, gourmet dining, and celebrity spotting in this duty-free paradise. The island combines French elegance with tropical beauty and yacht culture. Perfect for luxury travelers seeking sophisticated Caribbean experiences.
