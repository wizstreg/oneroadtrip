# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Spring (March-May)::** Perfect weather (20-25°C) with comfortable temperatures, blooming flowers, and excellent conditions for outdoor activities. Ideal for hiking, sightseeing, and cultural exploration.

**Autumn (September-November)::** Excellent temperatures after summer heat with stable weather and fewer typhoons. Perfect for all activities, cultural events, and comfortable exploration.

**Winter (December-February)::** Cool temperatures (15-20°C) in north, warmer in south, with minimal rainfall. Good for hot springs, indoor attractions, and southern regions.

**Summer (June-August)::** Hot, humid conditions (25-35°C) with typhoon season and heavy rainfall. Challenging weather but vibrant night markets and indoor attractions.

## Unique Experiences
Asian tiger with incredible diversity, traditional culture, and modern innovation. Experience night markets, beautiful landscapes, and authentic Chinese culture. The country offers cultural immersion with natural beauty and technological advancement. Taiwan provides Asian experiences with cultural richness and modern sophistication.
