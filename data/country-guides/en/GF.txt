# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (August-November)::** Best weather conditions, minimal rainfall, ideal for outdoor activities, peak tourist season, comfortable temperatures.

**Short Dry Season (February-March)::** Pleasant weather break, fewer crowds, good for cultural activities, moderate conditions.

**Wet Season (December-July)::** Heavy rainfall, high humidity, challenging conditions, lush rainforest, fewer tourists, lower prices.

**Space Launch Season::** Year-round launches, advance booking essential, unique experiences, international visitors.

## Unique Experiences
South American wilderness with European standards, space center, and incredible biodiversity. Experience rainforest adventures, traditional cultures, and unique colonial heritage. This territory offers authentic South American experiences with French infrastructure and amenities. French Guiana provides wilderness adventures with modern conveniences and cultural diversity.
