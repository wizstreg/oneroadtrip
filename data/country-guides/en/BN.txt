# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (February-April)::** Best weather conditions, minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures, ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

**Shoulder Months (May-June, December-January)::** Moderate rainfall, good weather between wet periods, fewer crowds, reasonable conditions.

**Wet Season (July-January)::** Heavy rainfall, high humidity, monsoon conditions, lush green landscapes, indoor cultural activities preferred.

**Ramadan Period::** Varies annually, limited dining options during day, special evening atmosphere, cultural experience but planning required.

## Unique Experiences
Islamic sultanate with pristine rainforests, golden mosques, and unique Malay culture. Experience traditional water villages, incredible biodiversity, and authentic Southeast Asian culture without crowds. The country offers luxury Islamic architecture and untouched natural environments. Brunei provides cultural authenticity and natural beauty in compact, accessible format.
