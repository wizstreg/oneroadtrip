# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Less rainfall with more stable weather conditions for boat transport. Optimal time for irregular shipping schedules and outdoor activities on coral atolls.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Higher rainfall and tropical storm risk affecting already irregular transportation. Challenging travel conditions but lush vegetation and authentic tropical experience.

**Shipping Schedule::** MV Lady Naomi operates monthly from Apia, Samoa, weather permitting. No fixed schedules, advance booking essential, cancellations common.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific paradise with pristine atolls, traditional culture, and sustainable living practices. Experience authentic island life, incredible marine environments, and Polynesian hospitality. The territory offers cultural authenticity with natural beauty and environmental leadership. Tokelau provides genuine Pacific experiences with cultural preservation and natural wonders.
