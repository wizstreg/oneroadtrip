# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures, clear skies, ideal touring, peak tourist season, advance booking recommended.

**Shoulder Season (April & November)::** Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate temperatures, reasonable prices, transitional seasons, excellent value.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Heavy rainfall, tropical storms, cyclone risk, high humidity, lowest prices, lush landscapes, challenging conditions.

**Cool Season (June-August)::** Coolest temperatures, dry conditions, comfortable weather, ideal hiking, clear skies, best overall conditions.

## Unique Experiences
Indian Ocean paradise with volcanic landscapes, unique culture, and pristine marine environments. Experience traditional perfume production, incredible diving, and authentic island life. The country offers cultural authenticity with natural beauty and minimal tourism development. Comoros provides genuine island experiences with cultural richness and natural preservation.
