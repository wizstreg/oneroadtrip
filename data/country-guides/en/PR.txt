# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Winter (December-April)::** Peak season with perfect weather (22-28°C), minimal rainfall, and ideal conditions for beaches and outdoor activities. Highest prices and crowds but most comfortable temperatures and lowest humidity levels.

**Late Spring (May)::** Excellent weather with moderate temperatures, fewer crowds, and reasonable prices. Weather becoming more humid but still comfortable, good value before summer heat arrives.

**Early Autumn (November)::** Pleasant temperatures after hurricane season, fewer tourists, and good value. Weather stabilizing after potential storm season, excellent for cultural exploration and outdoor activities.

**Summer (June-October)::** Hot, humid conditions (25-32°C) with hurricane season risk and afternoon thunderstorms. Lower prices but challenging weather, authentic Caribbean experience with lush green landscapes.

## Unique Experiences
Caribbean paradise with American amenities, Spanish heritage, and vibrant culture. Experience bioluminescent bays, beautiful beaches, and authentic Caribbean atmosphere. The territory offers cultural diversity with modern conveniences and natural beauty. Puerto Rico provides Caribbean experiences with American standards and cultural authenticity.
