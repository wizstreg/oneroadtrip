# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (April-September)::** Best weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (20-28Â°C), and excellent conditions for wildlife viewing and beach activities. Peak season for tourism.

**Cool Dry Season (May-August)::** Ideal temperatures and low humidity perfect for outdoor activities, cultural exploration, and marine activities. Best time for diving with excellent visibility.

**Hot Dry Season (September-November)::** Warmer temperatures but still good conditions, excellent for beach activities. Wildlife viewing good as animals concentrate around water sources.

**Rainy Season (December-March)::** Hot, humid weather with heavy rains and potential cyclones. Lush landscapes, fewer tourists, lower prices, but challenging travel conditions and cyclone risks.
