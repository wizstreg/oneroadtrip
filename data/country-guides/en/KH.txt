# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Cool Dry Season (November-February)::** Perfect weather, comfortable temperatures, minimal rainfall, ideal touring, clear skies, peak tourist season, advance booking recommended.

**Hot Season (March-May)::** Hot temperatures, dry conditions, fewer crowds, reasonable prices, early morning activities, challenging midday heat.

**Rainy Season (June-October)::** Heavy rainfall, lush landscapes, muddy roads, lowest prices, fewer tourists, challenging travel, dramatic storms.

**Best Temple Visiting (November-March)::** Comfortable temperatures, clear skies, optimal photography, manageable heat, ideal conditions, peak season.

## Unique Experiences
Ancient Khmer empire with Angkor Wat temples, rich history, and warm hospitality. Experience archaeological wonders, traditional crafts, and authentic Southeast Asian culture. The country offers cultural depth with natural beauty and historical significance. Cambodia provides transformative cultural experiences with archaeological treasures and cultural resilience.
