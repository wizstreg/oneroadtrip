# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-May)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, peak tourist season, highest prices, trade winds cooling.

**Shoulder Season (June-July, November)::** Good weather with occasional showers, fewer crowds, moderate prices, excellent value.

**Wet Season (August-October)::** Hurricane season, higher humidity, potential storms, lowest prices, lush landscapes, fewer tourists.

**Spice Harvest Season::** September-February nutmeg harvest, cultural significance, aromatic landscapes, authentic experiences.

## Unique Experiences
"Spice Island" with pristine beaches, nutmeg plantations, and authentic Caribbean culture. Experience underwater sculpture parks, traditional festivals, and warm hospitality. The island offers cultural authenticity with beautiful natural environments and culinary heritage. Grenada provides genuine Caribbean experiences with minimal mass tourism and cultural preservation.
