# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY WARNING:** Exercise caution due to petty crime, armed robbery in urban areas, and limited medical infrastructure. Avoid displaying valuables, use reputable accommodations, stay in groups after dark, and be particularly careful in Freetown and remote areas. Health risks include malaria, yellow fever, and tropical diseases. Road conditions poor with accident risks. Emergency services limited outside main cities.

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-April)::** Best weather conditions (24-32Â°C) with minimal rainfall, excellent beach weather, and optimal conditions for outdoor activities. Peak tourist season with higher prices but most comfortable temperatures and best road conditions.

**Harmattan Season (December-February)::** Cool, dry winds from Sahara create most pleasant temperatures with clear skies and excellent visibility. Best period for hiking, cultural exploration, and beach activities.

**Shoulder Season (May, October)::** Transitional periods with moderate rainfall, fewer crowds, and reasonable prices. Good compromise between weather and costs with acceptable conditions for most activities.

**Wet Season (May-October)::** Heavy rainfall with high humidity, challenging road conditions, and potential flooding. Lower prices but difficult travel conditions, lush green landscapes, and authentic tropical experience.
