# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-February)::** Less rainfall, more comfortable temperatures, better road conditions, ideal for travel, fewer mosquitoes, peak season.

**Shoulder Season (March-May & September-November)::** Moderate rainfall, good weather, fewer crowds, reasonable prices, transitional seasons, acceptable conditions.

**Wet Season (June-August)::** Heavy rainfall, high humidity, challenging travel, muddy roads, lush landscapes, lowest prices, fewer visitors.

**Year-Round Considerations::** Tropical climate, high humidity always, minimal temperature variation, weather unpredictable, proper preparation essential.

## Unique Experiences
Central African nation with Spanish colonial heritage, oil wealth, and pristine rainforests. Experience unique cultural mixing, incredible biodiversity, and authentic African traditions. The country offers minimal tourism with natural beauty and cultural diversity. Equatorial Guinea provides authentic African experiences with unique colonial heritage.
