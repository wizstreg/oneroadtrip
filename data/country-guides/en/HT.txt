# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-March)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, comfortable conditions, peak tourist season, best road conditions, advance planning recommended.

**Shoulder Season (April & November)::** Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate temperatures, reasonable conditions, occasional showers, transitional seasons.

**Hurricane Season (June-November)::** Heavy rainfall, storm risk, challenging conditions, lowest visitor numbers, infrastructure challenges, weather monitoring essential.

**Cooler Months (December-February)::** Most comfortable temperatures, dry conditions, clear skies, optimal travel conditions, cultural events, festive atmosphere.

## Unique Experiences
Caribbean authenticity with rich history, vibrant culture, and artistic heritage. Experience Vodou traditions, incredible art, and authentic Caribbean culture. The country offers cultural depth with natural beauty and historical significance. Haiti provides authentic Caribbean experiences with cultural richness and artistic traditions.
