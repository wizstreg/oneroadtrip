# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, trade winds, peak tourist season, highest prices, excellent for all activities.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Higher humidity, occasional tropical storms, cyclone season, fewer crowds, lower prices, lush landscapes.

**Shoulder Seasons (April-May, October-November)::** Good weather balance, moderate crowds, reasonable prices, ideal compromise.

**Year-Round Destination::** Tropical climate, consistently warm (75-85Â°F), cooling trade winds, excellent for beach activities always.
