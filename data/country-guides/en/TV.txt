# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Less rainfall with trade winds providing some cooling, but still hot and humid conditions. Better weather for limited outdoor activities and inter-atoll transportation.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Higher rainfall and tropical storm risk with very challenging conditions. Limited transportation between atolls, flooding possible, but fewer visitors.

**Flight Schedule::** Fiji Airways operates twice-weekly flights from Suva, Fiji with extremely limited capacity requiring months of advance booking.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific island paradise with pristine atolls, traditional culture, and climate change awareness. Experience authentic island life, incredible fishing, and Polynesian hospitality. The country offers cultural authenticity with natural beauty and environmental challenges. Tuvalu provides genuine Pacific experiences with cultural preservation and environmental awareness.
