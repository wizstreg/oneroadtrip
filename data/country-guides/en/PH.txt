# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY NOTE:** The Philippines is generally safe in main tourist areas, though avoid southern Mindanao and Sulu archipelago due to terrorism and kidnapping risks. Stick to established tourist destinations like Luzon, Visayas, and northern Mindanao. Check current government advisories and use reputable transportation and accommodation.

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-May)::** Peak tourist season with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (26-32Â°C), and excellent conditions for island hopping and beach activities. Highest prices and crowds.

**Cool Dry Season (December-February)::** Most comfortable temperatures and lowest humidity. Perfect weather for all activities, though expect highest accommodation rates and tourist numbers.

**Hot Dry Season (March-May)::** Hottest temperatures but still dry conditions. Good for beach activities, though midday heat can be intense. Shoulder season pricing.

**Rainy Season (June-November)::** Monsoon season with typhoon risk, but lower prices and fewer tourists. Some islands remain accessible, afternoon rains common.
