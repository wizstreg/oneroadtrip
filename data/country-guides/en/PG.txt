# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Best weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (24-30°C), and better road conditions. Peak season for trekking and cultural tours when accessible.

**Shoulder Season (April, November)::** Good weather with moderate rainfall, fewer tourists, and reasonable prices. Still suitable for most activities with proper planning.

**Wet Season (December-March)::** High rainfall, hot humid conditions (26-34°C), and challenging road access. Many areas become impassable, though cultural activities continue.

**Regional Variations::** Highlands cooler year-round, coastal areas hot and humid, islands have tropical maritime climate with trade wind moderation.

## Unique Experiences
Cultural diversity with traditional tribes, pristine rainforests, and incredible biodiversity. Experience authentic cultures, adventure activities, and natural beauty. The country offers cultural immersion with natural preservation and traditional authenticity. Papua New Guinea provides life-changing cultural experiences with natural wonders and cultural diversity.
