# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (June-September)::** Best weather conditions (22-28°C) with minimal rainfall, excellent conditions for hiking, beaches, and outdoor activities. Peak tourist season with moderate prices and most comfortable temperatures.

**Shoulder Seasons (April-May, October-November)::** Good weather with occasional brief showers, fewer crowds, and reasonable prices. Excellent for eco-tourism and cultural exploration with stable conditions.

**Wet Season (October-March)::** Higher rainfall with increased humidity but lush green landscapes and active waterfalls. Lower prices but weather limitations, authentic tropical experience with dramatic scenery.

**Year-Round Possibility::** Equatorial climate with consistent temperatures making visits possible throughout year, though seasonal rainfall affects hiking conditions and outdoor activities.

## Unique Experiences
Atlantic paradise with pristine nature, Portuguese heritage, and traditional culture. Experience chocolate plantations, beautiful beaches, and authentic island life. The country offers cultural authenticity with natural beauty and colonial heritage. São Tomé and Príncipe provide African experiences with tropical paradise and cultural preservation.
