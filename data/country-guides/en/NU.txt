# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (22-26°C), and trade winds. Best conditions for diving, whale watching, and outdoor activities.

**Whale Season (July-October)::** Prime time for humpback whale watching with excellent opportunities to swim with whales. Peak tourist season with advance booking essential.

**Shoulder Season (April-May, November)::** Good weather with occasional showers, moderate accommodation rates, and fewer crowds. Excellent value while maintaining comfortable conditions.

**Cyclone Season (November-April)::** Hot, humid weather (24-30°C) with potential tropical storms. Lower accommodation rates but weather monitoring essential.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific paradise with pristine coral reefs, traditional culture, and incredible diving. Experience authentic island life, whale watching, and Polynesian hospitality. The country offers untouched natural beauty with cultural authenticity and marine preservation. Niue provides genuine Pacific experiences with natural wonders and cultural preservation.
