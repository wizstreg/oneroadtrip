# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Winter (November-March)::** Perfect weather conditions (18-28°C) with pleasant temperatures, minimal rainfall, and ideal conditions for outdoor activities. Peak tourist season with highest prices but most comfortable climate for sightseeing and desert excursions.

**Spring (April-May)::** Excellent temperatures before summer heat with moderate humidity and stable weather patterns. Good value season with pleasant conditions for cultural exploration and outdoor adventures.

**Autumn (October)::** Cooling temperatures after extreme summer heat with decreasing humidity and returning outdoor activity possibilities. Excellent transition period with moderate prices and improving weather conditions.

**Summer (June-September)::** Extremely hot conditions (35-45°C) with high humidity and minimal outdoor comfort during daylight hours. Lowest prices but challenging weather requiring extensive air conditioning and indoor activities.
