# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, clear skies, peak tourist season, best road conditions, advance booking recommended.

**Shoulder Season (November & May)::** Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate temperatures, reasonable prices, occasional showers, excellent value, transitional seasons.

**Wet Season (June-October)::** Heavy rainfall, lush landscapes, muddy roads, hurricane risk, lowest prices, fewer tourists, challenging travel conditions.

**Bay Islands Season::** Year-round diving, consistent temperatures, trade wind cooling, dry season optimal, hurricane season awareness, marine activities ideal.
