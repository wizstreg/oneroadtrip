# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Pre-Monsoon (March-May)::** Excellent weather with clear mountain views, blooming rhododendrons, and warm temperatures (15-25°C). Peak trekking season with best Himalayan visibility.

**Post-Monsoon (September-November)::** Ideal conditions with crystal-clear mountain views, comfortable temperatures (10-20°C), and stable weather. Peak tourist season for trekking and cultural tours.

**Winter (December-February)::** Cool, dry weather (5-15°C) perfect for lower altitude activities and cultural exploration. Clear mountain views but cold temperatures at elevation.

**Monsoon Season (June-August)::** Hot, humid weather with heavy rains, leeches on trails, but lush landscapes and fewer tourists. Lower prices but challenging trekking conditions.

## Unique Experiences
Himalayan kingdom with Mount Everest, ancient temples, and spiritual traditions. Experience trekking adventures, traditional culture, and authentic spiritual experiences. The country offers adventure activities with cultural immersion and spiritual depth. Nepal provides transformative experiences with natural wonders and spiritual enrichment.
