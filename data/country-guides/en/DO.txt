# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Peak Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal beach conditions, highest prices, most crowded, advance booking essential.

**Shoulder Season (May-June, November)::** Good weather with occasional showers, fewer crowds, moderate prices, excellent value.

**Hurricane Season (July-October)::** Higher humidity, potential storms, lowest prices, lush landscapes, September-October peak risk.

**Baseball Season (October-February)::** Winter baseball league, cultural experience, passionate fans, authentic Dominican atmosphere.

## Unique Experiences
Caribbean's most diverse destination with pristine beaches, merengue music, and colonial heritage. Experience Santo Domingo's history, Punta Cana resorts, and authentic Dominican culture. The country offers excellent value with diverse landscapes and warm hospitality. Dominican Republic provides complete Caribbean experiences with cultural authenticity and beach paradise.
