# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-May)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, peak tourist season, excellent wildlife viewing, advance booking recommended.

**Cool Dry Season (November-February)::** Best weather, comfortable temperatures, perfect conditions, highest prices, crowded attractions, excellent value.

**Hot Dry Season (March-May)::** Hot temperatures, dry conditions, fewer crowds, reasonable prices, wildlife concentrated, good photography.

**Wet Season (June-October)::** Heavy rainfall, lush landscapes, lowest prices, fewer tourists, muddy conditions, challenging travel.

## Unique Experiences
Smallest African mainland country with incredible birdlife, river culture, and warm hospitality. Experience traditional music, river excursions, and authentic West African culture. The country offers cultural authenticity with natural beauty and stable democracy. Gambia provides accessible African experiences with cultural richness and natural diversity.
