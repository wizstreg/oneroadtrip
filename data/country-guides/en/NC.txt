# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (20-26°C), and trade winds. Peak tourist season with best conditions for outdoor activities.

**Cool Season (June-August)::** Ideal temperatures and low humidity, excellent for hiking and cultural exploration. Best time for whale watching and outdoor adventures.

**Shoulder Season (April-May, October-November)::** Good weather with occasional showers, moderate prices, and fewer crowds. Excellent value while maintaining comfortable conditions.

**Hot Season (November-April)::** Warm, humid weather (24-30°C) with potential cyclones, but lower prices and lush landscapes. Swimming season with warmest ocean temperatures.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific paradise with pristine lagoons, unique culture, and French sophistication. Experience traditional Kanak culture, incredible diving, and beautiful natural environments. The territory offers cultural diversity with natural beauty and modern amenities. New Caledonia provides Pacific experiences with cultural richness and natural wonders.
