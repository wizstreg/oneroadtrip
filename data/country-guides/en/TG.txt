# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY WARNING:** Generally safe but exercise caution due to petty crime in cities, occasional political tensions, and limited infrastructure. Avoid large gatherings, don't display valuables, use reputable accommodations, and be cautious when traveling alone at night. Health risks include malaria and tropical diseases.
