# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-March)::** Best weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (20-32°C), and reduced humidity. Peak season for any tourism activities when accessible.

**Cool Harmattan Season (December-February)::** Driest period with dusty winds from Sahara creating hazy conditions but cooler temperatures. Best time for northern regions when security permits.

**Hot Dry Season (March-May)::** Very hot temperatures (30-40°C) making travel challenging, especially in northern regions. Limited tourist activities due to extreme heat.

**Rainy Season (April-October)::** Heavy rainfall, high humidity, flooding possible in some areas. Many roads become difficult, though temperatures moderate and landscapes lush.

## Unique Experiences
West African giant with incredible diversity, vibrant culture, and economic dynamism. Experience Nollywood entertainment, traditional festivals, and authentic African experiences. The country offers cultural immersion with natural beauty and modern development. Nigeria provides authentic African experiences with cultural richness and economic vitality.

## Safety Warning
**SAFETY WARNING::** **SAFETY WARNING:** Nigeria has significant security risks including terrorism (especially in northeast), kidnapping threats, violent crime, and civil unrest. Many governments advise against travel to large areas, particularly northern regions and Niger Delta. Lagos and Abuja have tourism infrastructure but require extreme caution, professional security advice, and comprehensive insurance. Avoid remote areas and use only reputable operators.
