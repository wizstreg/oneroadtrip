# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-March, July-August)::** Best weather overall, peak tourist season, higher prices, ideal for most activities.

**Wet Season (April-June, September-November)::** More rainfall, fewer crowds, lower prices, lush landscapes, some areas challenging.

**Regional Variations::** Caribbean coast hot year-round, Andes cooler, Amazon humid, Pacific coast rainier, plan by region.

**Year-Round Destination::** Equatorial location means consistent temperatures, seasonal rainfall variations, eternal spring in many areas.

## Unique Experiences
Emerald landscapes, coffee culture, and vibrant cities create South America's most exciting destination. Experience Cartagena's colonial beauty, Amazon rainforest, and incredible biodiversity. The country offers salsa dancing, incredible hospitality, and diverse ecosystems from coast to mountains. Colombia combines natural beauty with passionate culture and improving safety.
