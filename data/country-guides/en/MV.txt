# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather with minimal rainfall, low humidity, and consistent sunshine. Peak tourist season with highest resort rates but ideal conditions for water activities and relaxation.

**Shoulder Season (May, November)::** Good weather with occasional brief showers, moderate resort rates, and fewer crowds. Excellent value while maintaining comfortable conditions for most activities.

**Wet Season (May-October)::** Higher rainfall and humidity with potential storms, but significantly lower resort rates and lush landscapes. Many resorts offer attractive packages during this period.

**Surfing Season (March-October)::** Southwest swells create excellent surfing conditions, particularly in southern atolls. Peak surf season coincides with wet season, offering unique experiences.

## Unique Experiences
Ultimate tropical paradise with overwater villas, pristine coral reefs, and luxury resorts. Experience perfect beaches, incredible diving, and romantic atmosphere. The country offers luxury experiences with natural beauty and peaceful environment. Maldives provides ultimate relaxation with natural wonders and sophisticated amenities.
