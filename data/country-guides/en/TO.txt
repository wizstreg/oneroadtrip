# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (May-October)::** Perfect weather (20-26°C) with minimal rainfall, trade winds, and excellent conditions for whale watching and outdoor activities. Peak season with comfortable temperatures and optimal diving conditions.

**Whale Season (July-November)::** Prime time for humpback whale watching and swimming experiences. Peak wildlife season with spectacular marine encounters and professional tour operators.

**Wet Season (November-April)::** Hot, humid conditions (24-30°C) with tropical storms and cyclone risk. Lower prices but weather limitations, lush landscapes, fewer tourists.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific kingdom with traditional culture, pristine nature, and authentic Polynesian experiences. Experience royal heritage, incredible marine life, and genuine island hospitality. The country offers cultural authenticity with natural beauty and traditional preservation. Tonga provides Polynesian experiences with cultural richness and natural wonders.
