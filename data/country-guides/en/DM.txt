# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-April)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal for hiking and outdoor activities, peak tourist season, higher prices.

**Shoulder Season (May-June, November)::** Good weather with occasional showers, fewer crowds, moderate prices, excellent value.

**Wet Season (July-October)::** Hurricane season, heavy rainfall, challenging hiking conditions, lowest prices, lush landscapes, potential storms.

**Whale Watching Season::** Year-round excellent, sperm whales resident, humpback whales winter months, best marine wildlife viewing.

## Unique Experiences
"Nature Island" with pristine rainforests, whale watching, and authentic Caribbean culture. Experience boiling lakes, incredible hiking, and traditional communities without mass tourism. The island offers sustainable eco-tourism with incredible biodiversity and warm hospitality. Dominica provides authentic Caribbean experiences with minimal commercial development and maximum natural beauty.
