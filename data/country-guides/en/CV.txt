# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (November-July)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal for beaches and outdoor activities, peak tourist season, trade winds cooling.

**Harmattan Season (December-March)::** Dry desert winds, excellent weather, best time overall, higher prices, perfect conditions.

**Wet Season (August-October)::** Brief rainy season, higher humidity, fewer crowds, lower prices, occasional tropical storms possible.
