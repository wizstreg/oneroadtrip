# Travel Guide: Complete Travel Guide

## Best Time to Visit
**Dry Season (December-June)::** Perfect weather, minimal rainfall, ideal temperatures, trade winds, peak tourist season, highest prices, advance booking recommended.

**Wet Season (July-November)::** Typhoon season, heavy rainfall, high humidity, fewer tourists, lowest prices, lush landscapes, weather monitoring essential.

**Shoulder Season (October-November & January)::** Good weather, fewer crowds, moderate temperatures, reasonable prices, excellent value, transitional seasons.

**Year-Round Tropical::** Consistent temperatures, high humidity, ocean breezes, minimal seasonal variation, always swimming weather, pack accordingly.

## Unique Experiences
Pacific paradise with American amenities, Chamorro culture, and World War II history. Experience tropical beauty with modern conveniences and authentic Pacific island culture. The territory offers excellent shopping with cultural diversity and natural beauty. Guam provides American standards with Pacific paradise and cultural authenticity.
