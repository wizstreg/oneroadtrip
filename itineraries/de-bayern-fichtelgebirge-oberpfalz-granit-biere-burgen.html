2

⏱ 15 min

Activities

Take the Seilbahn cable car from Bischofsgrün to climb the 250-meter elevation gain without breaking a sweat. In summer, the Ochsenkopf Coaster—a kilometer-long toboggan track on rails—races downhill at 40 km/h. Family thrills guaranteed, individual braking.
