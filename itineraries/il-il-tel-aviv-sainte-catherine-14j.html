Israel and Sinai Highlights: Sacred Sites and Timeless Desert Landscapes

📅 14 days📍 12 stops🚗 1084 km⏱ 16h44

Tel Aviv → Haifa → Acre → Tiberias → Nazareth → Majdal Shams → Jerusalem → Bethleem → Ein Bokek → Mitzpe Ramon → Eilat → Sainte Catherine

Feel Tel Aviv's pulse between beaches and urban life, explore Haifa and its blended cultures, then travel back in time to Acre and its Crusader fortress. Follow the Sea of Galilee, discover Nazareth and Majdal Shams, sense the intensity of Jerusalem and Bethlehem, relax at Ein Bokek and walk the mineral expanses of Mitzpe Ramon.

☀️ What weather should you expect?

Spring (March-May): Perfect weather, mild temperatures, blooming landscapes, ideal conditions, comfortable touring, fewer crowds, excellent value.

Fall (September-November): Excellent weather, warm days, cool evenings, comfortable temperatures, harvest season, fewer tourists, good value.

Summer (June-August): Hot weather, peak tourist season, highest prices, crowded attractions, beach season, festival time, advance booking essential.

Winter (December-February): Mild weather, occasional rain, lowest prices, fewer crowds, comfortable temperatures, cultural focus, cozy atmosphere.

🌍 What to discover in IL?

Holy Land with ancient history, modern innovation, and cultural diversity. Experience religious sites, incredible cuisine, and authentic Middle Eastern culture.
