Salzwedel

Home of the legendary Baumkuchen spit cake since centuries

★★☆☆☆
1

Savor Salzwedel, the town of Baumkuchen, where this traditional spit cake has been made according to ancestral recipes for centuries. This sweet specialty with its multiple golden layers, slowly cooked on a rotating cylinder, has become the culinary emblem of the Altmark. The old Hanseatic town with its half-timbered houses and Gothic brick churches provides a charming setting for this gourmet discovery.

⏱ 75 min
2

Cruise on Lake Arendsee aboard the paddle steamer Queen Arendsee, which glides peacefully over the waters of this 5 km² natural lake. This nostalgic cruise evokes the atmosphere of old-time steamboats while offering enchanting panoramas of the wooded shores. The lake, the largest in the Altmark, is a popular destination for relaxation and water activities.

⏱ 75 min
3

Discover the singular story of Gustav Nagel, legendary itinerant preacher and natural healer of the early 20th century. This eccentric figure lived in a cabin he had built near Lake Arendsee, traveling the region barefoot to spread his messages of peace and communion with nature. His legacy bears witness to the spiritual allure this peaceful region already held.

⏱ 75 min

Activities

Halle

Handel's birthplace and guardian of the mysterious Nebra Sky Disc

★★★☆☆
1

Discover Halle-on-Saale, birthplace of the famous composer George Frideric Handel whose monument proudly adorns the market square. This 3,600-year-old bronze disc, adorned with gold representing the sun, moon, and stars, revolutionized our understanding of Bronze Age astronomical knowledge. The Francke Foundations, a Baroque architectural complex dedicated to Pietist education, complete this exceptional heritage.

Brocken

Northern Germany's highest peak shrouded in legend and mist

★★★★☆
1

Conquer the Brocken, Saxony-Anhalt's highest point and the highest mountain in northern Germany at 1,141 meters elevation. This mythical summit, shrouded in mist and legend, is the gathering place of witches during Walpurgis Night according to Germanic folklore. On clear days, panoramas from the summit extend up to 200 kilometers, embracing the northern plains and neighboring massifs.

⏱ 75 min
2

Board the Harzer Brockenbahn steam train, one of Germany's last narrow-gauge railways still in regular service. This historic line climbs the slopes of the Brocken through fir forests and highland peat bog landscapes in a nostalgic atmosphere. The 90-minute train journey constitutes an experience as memorable as the destination itself.

⏱ 75 min
3

Discover Heinrich Heine Rock, named in honor of the famous poet who climbed the Brocken in 1820 and immortalized this experience in his travel narrative. Hiking trails wind through a subarctic landscape unique in central Germany, where stunted vegetation testifies to the extreme climatic conditions. The delicious Brockensplitter chocolates are the gourmet reward for this ascent.

⏱ 75 min

Stolberg

Preserved medieval charm and the world's largest iron cross

★★☆☆☆
1

Explore Stolberg, a picturesque town in the southern Harz whose medieval center is miraculously preserved. Historic half-timbered houses lining the steep alleyways create a setting from another time at the foot of the castle overlooking the valley. Stolberg Castle, residence of the eponymous counts, and St. Martin's Church constitute the emblematic symbols of this romantic town.

⏱ 75 min
2

Discover the birthplace of Thomas Müntzer, radical theologian and leader of the Peasants' War born here in 1489. A statue commemorates this controversial figure who defied the established order in the name of social justice. His revolutionary legacy makes Stolberg an important memorial site for the history of social movements in Europe.

⏱ 75 min
3

Climb the 200 steps of Joseph's Cross, the world's largest iron double cross erected on the Großer Auerberg. This impressive 38-meter-high metal structure, built in 1896, offers a magnificent view of the Harz massif from its observation platform. The 360-degree panorama rewards the climbing effort with a grandiose natural spectacle.

⏱ 75 min
