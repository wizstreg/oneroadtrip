Namibia's Northern Wilderness: Damaraland to Kaokoland Safari Adventure

📅 12 days📍 10 stops🚗 1333 km⏱ 19h01

Follow Damaraland tracks where desert elephants cross russet valleys, then listen to Twyfelfontein and its petroglyphs carved in stone. Push toward Kaokoland and its Himba lands, before reaching Epupa Falls rumbling in a palm-lined gorge. Namibia offers a raw and human journey.

✨ Highlights
  • Twyfelfontein: bushman petroglyphs, sandstone
  • Epupa Falls: baobab thunder, kunene
  • Palmwag: desert elephant territory
  • Opuwo: himba heartland, ochre traditions
⏳ How many days should I plan?

To make the most of it, plan between 4 and 8 days.

☀️ What weather should you expect?

Dry Season (May-October): Best weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (15-25°C), and excellent conditions for wildlife viewing and desert exploration. Peak tourist season.

Cool Dry Season (May-August): Ideal temperatures, clear skies, and perfect conditions for outdoor activities. Best wildlife viewing as animals concentrate around water sources.

Hot Dry Season (September-October): Warmer days but still excellent conditions, particularly good for wildlife viewing. Desert temperatures can be extreme but landscapes spectacular.

Rainy Season (November-April): Hot temperatures (25-35°C) with brief afternoon showers creating lush landscapes. Fewer crowds, lower prices, but challenging road conditions.

Southern African gem with stunning deserts, incredible wildlife, and pristine landscapes. Experience Sossusvlei dunes, traditional cultures, and authentic African adventures.
