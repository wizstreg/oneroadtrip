📆 Best period

May, June, July, August, September, October

⏳ How many days should I plan?

To make the most of it, plan between 16 and 20 days.

☀️ What weather should you expect?

Dry Season (May-October): Best weather with minimal rainfall, comfortable temperatures (15-25°C), and excellent conditions for wildlife viewing and desert exploration. Peak tourist season.

Cool Dry Season (May-August): Ideal temperatures, clear skies, and perfect conditions for outdoor activities. Best wildlife viewing as animals concentrate around water sources.

Hot Dry Season (September-October): Warmer days but still excellent conditions, particularly good for wildlife viewing. Desert temperatures can be extreme but landscapes spectacular.

Rainy Season (November-April): Hot temperatures (25-35°C) with brief afternoon showers creating lush landscapes. Fewer crowds, lower prices, but challenging road conditions.

Southern African gem with stunning deserts, incredible wildlife, and pristine landscapes. Experience Sossusvlei dunes, traditional cultures, and authentic African adventures. Embark on the ultimate southern African overland journey from Namibia's towering red dunes to Botswana's waterlogged Okavango Delta. This self-drive 4x4 camping expedition crosses two countries, combining the world's oldest desert with Africa's largest inland delta. Navigate epic sand dunes at Sossusvlei, track desert elephants in Damaraland, encounter Etosha's legendary wildlife concentrations, then plunge into the lush wilderness of Chobe and Moremi where lions hunt in paradise.
