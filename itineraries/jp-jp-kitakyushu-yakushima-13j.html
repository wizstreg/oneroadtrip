Kyushu and Yakushima, Japan: Volcanoes, Hot Springs and Ancient Forests

📅 13 days📍 13 stops🚗 0 km⏱

Discover Beppu and its steaming springs, then gain height around Aso's crater above grassy plains. On Yakushima, walk beneath ancient cedars in a mossy forest, before reaching Nagasaki and its port memory. End with Takachiho and its mythic gorges, where water and rock tell Kyushu's story.

✨ Highlights
  • Yakushima: ancient cedars, moss cathedrals
  • Beppu: steaming hells, onsen paradise
  • Aso: smoking caldera, grass sea
  • Takachiho: mythical gorge, sacred dance
  • Nagasaki: history and harbor healing
⏳ How many days should I plan?

To make the most of it, plan between 12 and 16 days.

☀️ What weather should you expect?

Spring (March-May): Cherry blossom season, perfect weather, mild temperatures, iconic sakura viewing, peak tourist season, advance booking essential, magical atmosphere.

Fall (September-November): Autumn colors, comfortable weather, clear skies, beautiful foliage, excellent touring, fewer crowds, ideal photography, pleasant conditions.

Summer (June-August): Hot humid weather, rainy season, festival season, vibrant atmosphere, mountain hiking, beach activities, air conditioning essential.

Winter (December-February): Cold weather, snow sports, illuminations, onsen season, fewer crowds, winter festivals, cozy atmosphere, skiing opportunities.

🌍 What to discover in JP?

Unique culture with ancient traditions, modern innovation, and incredible attention to detail. Experience temples, technology, and authentic Japanese lifestyle. The country offers cultural immersion with natural beauty and culinary excellence. Japan provides transformative cultural experiences with historical depth and modern sophistication.
