New Zealand's Grand Circuit: North & South Island in 3 Weeks

📅 27 days📍 21 stops🚗 4322 km⏱ 63h10

Follow Rotorua and its geysers, then trace the East Cape coast where ocean strikes rock. Cross Cook Strait to the South Island, head to Abel Tasman and its golden beaches, then approach Milford Sound and its dizzying cliffs. New Zealand strings together scenes, each road offering a new idea of adventure.

✨ Highlights
  • Rotorua: geothermal wonderland Maori culture
  • Abel Tasman: golden bays kayak
  • Milford Sound: fjord cliffs waterfalls
  • Queenstown: adventure capital lakeside thrill
  • Mount Cook: glacier views alpine
⏳ How many days should I plan?

To make the most of it, plan between 13 and 17 days.

☀️ What weather should you expect?

Summer (December-February): Peak season with warmest weather (18-25°C), long daylight hours, and best conditions for hiking, beaches, and outdoor activities. Highest prices and crowds.

Autumn (March-May): Excellent weather with comfortable temperatures (12-22°C), stunning fall colors, and fewer tourists. Great value season with stable weather conditions.

Winter (June-August): Cool temperatures (5-15°C) perfect for skiing, fewer crowds, and lower accommodation rates. Best time for Southern Alps snow sports and cozy indoor experiences.

Spring (September-November): Mild weather (10-20°C) with blooming landscapes, moderate crowds, and good value. Ideal for hiking and outdoor activities before summer crowds arrive.

🌍 What to discover in NZ?

Adventure paradise with stunning landscapes, Maori culture, and outdoor activities. Experience Lord of the Rings scenery, traditional culture, and incredible natural beauty.
