Complete Botswana Circuit: 15-day 4x4 safari and camping adventure

📅 15 days📍 15 stops🚗 764 km⏱ 30h25

Maun → Moremi South Gate → Third Bridge → Khwai Magotho → Savuti Camp → Kasane → Victoria Falls

Maun → Moremi South Gate → Third Bridge → Khwai Magotho → Savuti Camp → Kasane → Victoria Falls

From Maun to Victoria Falls, traverse the wildest sanctuaries of Southern Africa. In complete autonomy aboard a fully-equipped 4x4, explore the Okavango Delta, track the great predators of Savuti, and finish facing the spray of Victoria Falls. An authentic adventure journey into the heart of the African bush.

Maun

★★★☆☆

Activities

Landing at Maun after a long flight via Johannesburg. The warm Kalahari air greets you on the dusty tarmac of this airport, which becomes one of Africa's busiest during peak season. Transfer to your accommodation for a first night of rest.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.7Phazama Farm

Maun

★★★☆☆
1

Full briefing at the 4x4 rental company. They explain every corner of the vehicle: the winch, compressor, jerrycans, rooftop tent setup, and sand tracks. Take notes, photograph the vehicle from all angles before signing.

⏱ 120 min

Activities

Strategic supplies run in Maun. Fill the fridge with fresh meat, the trunk with canned goods and pasta, the jerrycans with water. Last reliable grocery store before Kasane in 8 days. Don't underestimate the quantities.

⏱ 60 min

Scenic flight over the Okavango Delta in late afternoon. From the Cessna, the labyrinth of turquoise channels unfolds, dotted with elephant herds and palm islands. A unique perspective on this UNESCO-listed ecosystem.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.7Phazama Farm
🚗Drive to next stop: 100 km · 3h

Moremi South Gate

★★★☆☆
1

Check-in at Moremi Game Reserve's South Gate. Formalities completed, you pass through the barrier and enter one of Africa's most pristine sanctuaries. Tarmac gives way to sand. Welcome to the bush.

⏱ 30 min

Activities

First self-drive safari on Moremi's tracks. Eyes fixed on the sand, you learn to read the signs: lion prints, fresh elephant dung - the bush reveals its secrets. Giraffes, zebras, impalas appear between the mopanes.

⏱ 60 min

Setting up at South Gate campsite before nightfall. Rooftop tent assembly, first campfire under the southern stars. Hyenas prowl around the camp, their cackles piercing the darkness.

⏱ 60 min
$$8.5Oasis Hotel
🚗Drive to next stop: 52 km · 2h30

Third Bridge

★★★★☆
1

Route to Third Bridge, the legendary campsite in the heart of the marshes. The track crosses First Bridge then Second Bridge - the latter in poor condition, detour sometimes necessary. Expect about a hundred water pools to cross after rain.

⏱ 150 min

Activities

Safari in the Third Bridge wetland area. Lagoons covered in water lilies harbor hippos and crocodiles. Lechwe bound through the shallow water. On the islands, buffalo herds are regularly hunted by lions.

⏱ 60 min

Setting up at Third Bridge Camp, one of Moremi's most beautiful sites. Sites 8, 9 and 10 offer more privacy and spectacular marsh views. Baboons in the sausage trees above camp - don't leave anything lying around.

⏱ 60 min
🚗Drive to next stop: 35 km · 2h

Khwai Magotho

★★★★☆
1

Crossing Fourth Bridge - Moremi's longest bridge, in good condition - then exit via North Gate. You leave the national park to enter the Khwai Community Concession, a territory managed by the villagers.

⏱ 30 min

Activities

Safari in the Khwai Community Concession. This buffer zone between Moremi and Chobe offers exceptional wildlife density. Elephants by the hundreds, lions, leopards and especially the highly sought-after wild dogs that sometimes establish their dens in the area.

⏱ 60 min

Setting up at Magotho Camp (also called Khwai Community Camp), 20 km east of the village. Elephants cross the campsite constantly - a magical but intimidating experience the first few times. Stay calm, keep your distance.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.5Hideaways Mogotlho Safari Lodge, Khwai River
🚗Drive to next stop: 120 km · 5h

Khwai Magotho

★★★★☆

Activities

Early morning safari in the Khwai Concession at sunrise. Predators are still active, elephants head toward the river. Golden light illuminates the floodplains. Return to camp for breakfast.

⏱ 60 min

Mokoro excursion on the Mbudi River at midday. Traditional dugout canoes glide silently through the papyrus. Hippos, crocodiles, kingfishers. A peaceful interlude before the Savuti tracks.

⏱ 60 min

Guided night drive in the concession in late afternoon. Unlike national parks, Khwai permits night safaris. Headlights sweep the savanna searching for the glowing eyes of predators.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.5Hideaways Mogotlho Safari Lodge, Khwai River
🚗Drive to next stop: 120 km · 5h

Savuti Camp

★★★★☆
1

Early departure toward Savuti. Two possible routes: Sand Ridge Route (recommended in rainy season) or Marsh Route (better in dry season). Ask for advice at Savuti camp before leaving. Prepare for 4-5 hours of demanding track.

⏱ 300 min

Activities

Entering the Savuti region, southern part of Chobe National Park. Radically different landscape: dry plains, solitary bull elephants, massive baobabs. The Savuti Channel, dry for decades, has recently come back to life.

⏱ 60 min

Setting up at Savuti Camp, a small campsite of about 8 sites managed by SKL. The camp is often full - reservation essential. Sites 1-4 and Paradise Camp offer views of the channel. On-site shop for firewood and cold drinks.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.3Elephant Valley Lodge
🚗Drive to next stop: 50 km · 2h

Savuti Camp

★★★★☆

Activities

Morning safari on the Savuti plains. This region is famous for its lions that have learned to hunt elephants - a unique behavior observed nowhere else. Predator-prey interactions are spectacular.

⏱ 60 min

Rest at camp during the hot hours. Animals seek shade, the heat is crushing. Nap, reading, vehicle maintenance. SKL teams come by to clean vacant sites.

⏱ 60 min

Late afternoon safari around the waterholes. Elephants converge on the pans, predators start becoming active. The sun sets over golden plains. Return to camp before gate closure.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.3Elephant Valley Lodge
🚗Drive to next stop: 170 km · 7h

Kasane

★★★☆☆
1

Big track day: Savuti to Kasane. Count on 5 to 7 hours driving in deep sand. The route crosses mopane forests and very demanding sections. Take your time, respect the vehicle.

⏱ 420 min

Activities

Arriving in Kasane late afternoon. First town since Maun: supermarkets, gas stations, ATMs. Your body craves a hot shower and a proper meal. Full resupply for the final days.

⏱ 60 min

Night at a lodge or campsite in Kasane. After the grueling Savuti-Kasane track, a real night's rest is needed. Some lodges offer views of the Chobe River and passing elephants at dusk.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.3Elephant Valley Lodge
🚗Drive to next stop: 10 km · 15min

Kasane

★★★☆☆
1

Morning safari on the Chobe Riverfront. Entry via Sedudu Gate, 6 km from Kasane. The track runs along the river for kilometers. Here lies the world's largest elephant concentration: 50,000 to 120,000 individuals by some estimates.

⏱ 210 min

Activities

Elephants cross the river before you, trunks intertwined to protect the young from the current. Hippos emerge snorting. Buffaloes graze by the hundreds on the islands. Chobe Riverfront offers one of the world's best water-based safaris.

⏱ 60 min

Sunset photo cruise on the Chobe River. The boat glides between hippos, along banks where elephants come to drink. The sun sets the sky ablaze. Cocktail in hand, you savor one of Africa's most beautiful sunsets.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.3Elephant Valley Lodge
🚗Drive to next stop: 80 km · 3h

Victoria Falls

1

Border crossing at Kazungula. Endless line of trucks but cars pass faster. Formalities on the Botswana side, ferry or bridge across the Zambezi, then Zimbabwean immigration. Allow 45 minutes to 3 hours depending on traffic.

⏱ 120 min
2

Zimbabwean visa on arrival. 50 USD for multiple entry, practical if you plan to go back and forth. Cash payment only. Keep your dollars handy.

⏱ 30 min

Activities

Returning the 4x4 at Victoria Falls or Kasane per your contract. End of the self-drive adventure. Vehicle inspection, paperwork, transfer to your lodge. Luxury after two weeks in the bush.

⏱ 60 min

Settling in at your lodge in Victoria Falls Town. After two weeks of camping, the comfort of a real room, a pool and a restaurant feels wonderful. The town is compact, walkable, with a relaxed atmosphere.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.2Shongwe Lookout
🚗Drive to next stop: 2 km · 10min

Victoria Falls

1

Victoria Falls National Park, Zimbabwe side. Mosi-oa-Tunya - the smoke that thunders. 107 meters high, 1.7 km wide, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World. The thunder can be heard from miles away.

⏱ 180 min

Activities

Walking through the rainforest along the falls. The trail passes through a tropical forest created by the permanent spray. You will get soaked - it's unavoidable. Poncho and towel essential. Protect your camera.

⏱ 60 min

Burning Point (point 13) offers the most spectacular view - where Zimbabwe and Zambia meet. Nearly permanent double rainbow when the sun shines. Take your time, savor the spectacle.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.2Shongwe Lookout

Victoria Falls

Activities

Helicopter flight over the falls - the Flight of Angels. 13 to 25 minutes of pure magic. Aerial view of the curtain of water, the Zambezi gorges, Victoria Falls Bridge. Book the front seat for unobstructed views.

⏱ 60 min

Zipline above the Zambezi gorges from Lookout Café. 120 meters above the void, speeds up to 100 km/h. Stunning views of both sides of the falls. For thrill seekers.

⏱ 60 min

Sundowner at Victoria Falls Hotel or on the banks of the Zambezi. Colonial high tea, sunset cocktails, view of the falls spray in the distance. A touch of luxury to close out the adventure.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.2Shongwe Lookout

Victoria Falls

1

Buffer day - safety margin built into the itinerary. If a mechanical problem had occurred in the bush, this 24-hour buffer ensures you won't miss your international flight. Otherwise, enjoy!

⏱ 60 min

Activities

Relaxed morning at the lodge. Pool, spa, extended breakfast. After two intense weeks, body and mind appreciate the rest. Sort through your photos, start to realize what you've just experienced.

⏱ 60 min

Stroll through Victoria Falls Town and its craft markets. Wood carvings, basketwork, souvenirs. Negotiate with a smile. Elephant's Walk Shopping Village offers quality crafts.

⏱ 60 min

Dinner at Three Monkeys, a local institution. Wood-fired pizzas, creative cocktails, relaxed atmosphere. Open terrace, live music some evenings. Book ahead - it's often full.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.2Shongwe Lookout
🚗Drive to next stop: 25 km · 30min

Victoria Falls

1

Transfer to Victoria Falls Airport. The airport is small but efficient, 20 km from town. Arrive 2.5 hours before your international flight. Last-minute souvenir shopping in the duty-free shop.

⏱ 60 min

Activities

International return flight via Johannesburg, Addis Ababa or Nairobi. Botswana recedes beneath the aircraft wings. Images flash by: elephants crossing the Chobe, Savuti lions, Victoria Falls spray. The adventure ends, the memories remain.

⏱ 60 min
$$$9.2Shongwe Lookout
