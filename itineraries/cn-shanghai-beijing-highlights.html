China Road Trip: Shanghai to Beijing Highlights

📅 16 days📍 12 stops🚗 3395 km⏱ 38h50

Shanghai → Zhangjiajie → Xian → Beijing

Futuristic Shanghai, the floating peaks of Zhangjiajie that inspired Avatar, Xi'an's legendary Terracotta Army, and imperial Beijing. Experience China's most iconic wonders in one unforgettable journey.

✨ Highlights
  • The breathtaking sandstone pillars of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park
  • Standing among 8,000 life-sized Terracotta Warriors in Xi'an
  • Walking the Great Wall at sunrise from Mutianyu
  • Exploring the vast Forbidden City in Beijing
  • Cruising the Huangpu River past Shanghai's glittering skyline
  • Wandering through traditional hutong neighborhoods
📆 Best period

April, May, September, October

⏳ How many days should I plan?

To make the most of it, plan between 10 and 14 days.

☀️ What weather should you expect?

Spring (April-May): Pleasant temperatures, blooming flowers, ideal weather, moderate crowds, excellent for most regions, comfortable travel.

Fall (September-October): Perfect weather, clear skies, comfortable temperatures, peak tourist season, higher prices, autumn colors beautiful.

Summer (June-August): Hot and humid, rainy season, crowded tourist sites, highest prices, but best for Tibet and western regions.

Winter (November-March): Cold temperatures, fewer crowds, lower prices, perfect for southern regions, harsh in north, unique winter experiences.

Regional Variations: Vast climate diversity, southern subtropical, northern continental, western plateau, plan according to regions visited.

🌍 What to discover in CN?

Ancient civilization meets modern superpower with Great Wall, forbidden cities, and cutting-edge technology. Experience diverse landscapes, incredible cuisine, and 5000-year cultural heritage. The country offers bustling megacities, pristine nature, and traditional arts. China provides overwhelming cultural richness and geographical diversity in rapidly modernizing nation.
