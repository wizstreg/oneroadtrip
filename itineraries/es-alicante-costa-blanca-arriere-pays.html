Denia

Where UNESCO gastronomy meets the wild slopes of Montgó Natural Park.
Denia CastleMontgó Natural Park Denia
✈️ Book a flight🚗 Rent a car
1

A UNESCO Creative City of Gastronomy since 2015, Denia earned its stripes through its celebrated red prawn—the gamba roja—and its rice dishes rooted in Arab tradition. The 11th-century Almohad castle watches over the sea from its hilltop: built by successive occupiers, its walls blend Moorish architecture with Renaissance additions. Climb early in the morning when the raking light over the bay is at its finest. Paid entry, audioguide available.

⏱ 90 min
2

Orihuela often goes unnoticed on the road to Murcia, which is a mistake. Its historic center, declared an artistic monument in 1969, brings together El Salvador Cathedral (begun in the 14th century on the ruins of the Aljama mosque, elevated to cathedral in 1564), the convent of Santo Domingo, and the castle on Mount San Miguel. The cathedral preserves a Velázquez painting, The Temptation of Saint Thomas, in its sacred art museum.

Orihuela sits 10 km from the sea: Cabo Roig beach is the closest.

Torrevieja

Pink lagoon of halobacteria, Europe's largest salt producer.
Pink Lagoon TorreviejaTorrevieja Salt Flats Natural Park
✈️ Book a flight🚗 Rent a car
1

Vivid pink in summer, lilac in winter: the lagoon shifts with salt concentration and light. Its color is produced by halobacteria and the algae Dunaliella salina. At 350 grams of salt per liter, the concentration matches that of the Dead Sea. Pink flamingos feed here regularly. Swimming prohibited—but the walk along the greenway at sunset ranks among the finest on the Costa Blanca.

⏱ 60 min

Activities

The salt flats have produced salt since Roman times at this site—remains of a probable Roman factory were found in underwater excavations. Today Europe's largest salt producer, the complex extracts significant quantities annually, much of it sent to salt Northern European roads in winter. A tourist train departs from Paseo de la Libertad for a guided tour of the salt flats. Available in high season.
