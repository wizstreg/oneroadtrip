Discover Lausanne, the dynamic Olympic capital perched on the shores of Lake Geneva. Visit the Olympic Museum, a treasure trove of sports history showcasing the Olympic movement from ancient Greece to modern times. Interactive exhibits, authentic memorabilia, and the stunning lakeside setting make this one of Switzerland's most engaging museums.

⏱ 75 min
2

Climb to the Lausanne Cathedral, a stunning Gothic masterpiece and Switzerland's largest medieval church. The panoramic views from the top encompass the city, Lake Geneva, and on clear days, the distant peaks of the French Alps. A night watchman still calls out the hours from the tower, maintaining a tradition dating back centuries.
